About Me

I’m a software engineering student and developer who enjoys turning ideas into clean, working products. I’m especially interested in building applications that are simple to use, efficient under the hood, and actually solve real problems, not just look good on screenshots. I work across both front-end and back-end development, with experience designing user interfaces, writing server-side logic, and connecting everything together into a smooth experience. I enjoy breaking down complex problems, thinking through edge cases, and building systems that make sense to both users and developers. Beyond classes and coursework, I’m constantly learning, experimenting, and improving my skills through personal projects and real-world applications. I like collaborating with other developers, learning new tools, and pushing myself to build things that are better than my last project. My goal is simple: keep getting better, build meaningful software, and contribute to products that people actually enjoy using.
